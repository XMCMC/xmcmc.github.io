XMCMC is a general Bayesian modeling language and sampler that is block and cache optimized. It tends to outperform its competitors like Stan and JAGS, especially as model complexity grows.

Overview

XMCMC takes a minimalist modeling language perspective, so the language is easy to learn and use.
